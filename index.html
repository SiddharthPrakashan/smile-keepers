"We make a living by what we get.. But we make a life by what we give"

We the smile keepers have started a small initiative to spread love and smiles across Goa.

All we wish is to make this world a happy place.

We wish to visit orphanages, old age homes and many other places where we shall aim at making them smile with few little things that we can give ❤

We besides spending time with them also wish to teach them something that will be helpful for them in the long run.

This shall include enviromental awareness, personal hygiene, introduction to technology and lots more. .

We wish nothing but a little support from all those who wish to do so ❤
